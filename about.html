About Us

A nonprofit student-run, all female organisation aimed at empowering, connecting and inspiring young girls in STEM......

MISSION

To connect,inspire and empower young girls with technology skills to make great changes in their community.

VISION

A future where young girls will be in charge of the digital and technology world.

CORE VALUES

Our values are Team Work, Innovation, Leadership, Excellence , Creativity , Dedication and Growth.

Community

Our community helps girls from different areas in Africa feel a sense of belonging and have vital social connections, engagements and also boosts their physical and mental health.

Resources

We publish articles and videos to help you improve your growth and stay updated with latest technology news.
